📌 What is PredictX?

PredictX is a graduate admission prediction system built as a data science project. It uses a Linear Regression model trained on the Admission Predict Ver1.1 Dataset to predict the probability of a student getting admitted based on their academic profile.

Students can enter their GRE Score, TOEFL Score, University Rating, SOP, LOR, CGPA, and Research Experience — and get an instant admission probability percentage. The platform also provides interactive data visualizations to explore dataset insights.

🗂️ Dataset

The model is trained on the Admission Predict Ver1.1 Dataset which contains 500 student records with 8 columns including the target variable.

GRE Score (290–340)  ·  TOEFL Score (92–120)  ·  University Rating (1–5)  ·  SOP Strength (1–5)  ·  LOR Strength (1–5)  ·  CGPA (6.8–9.92)  ·  Research (0 or 1)  ·  Chance of Admit (0.34–0.97)

Out of 500 students, 280 have research experience and 220 do not. The average admission chance in the dataset is 72%.
